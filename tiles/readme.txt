RUNNING PROGRAM:
Run tiles.exe to execute program. Other files included for display of code.


INSTRUCTIONS:
Press a button 1-6 on the keyboard to place a tile.

The button you press represents the angle (360 * button pressed/14) in a circle 
of the side of the tile touching the center of the screen. Tiles will build around
clockwise from 12 O'clock around center of the screen.

To clear the screen press the spacebar.


OTHER:
Program for windows 8+ with DirectX, not guarenteed to work with other OS.
Program compiled statically, executable independant of libraries.

Compile with dependencies: tiles.cpp Graphics.cpp
Link with libraries: User32.Lib d2d1.lib
